Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing marketing campaign performance using the Bank Marketing Dataset through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The objective was to identify customer conversion patterns, analyze funnel drop-offs, evaluate communication channel effectiveness, and generate actionable insights to improve lead-to-customer conversion performance.

The dashboard was designed using a professional dark-theme executive layout to simulate a real-world business intelligence reporting environment.

🎯 Business Objectives
Analyze end-to-end marketing funnel performance
Identify major customer drop-off stages
Measure overall conversion effectiveness
Evaluate communication channel performance
Discover high-converting customer segments
Analyze customer demographics and behavioral trends
Generate data-driven recommendations to improve campaign efficiency

💡 Key Insights
Major customer drop-off occurs during the lead qualification stage
Cellular communication generated the highest customer conversion performance
Customers aged 26–45 showed stronger engagement and conversion behavior
Repeated campaign calls negatively impacted conversion probability
Management and technician professions contributed more successful conversions
Conversion performance varied significantly across campaign months
Higher education groups demonstrated better conversion trends
🚀 Key Takeaways
Improve lead qualification processes to reduce funnel drop-offs
Prioritize high-converting customer segments for targeted marketing
Focus more on cellular communication campaigns
Reduce excessive customer contact frequency
Implement data-driven customer targeting strategies
Optimize campaign timing based on monthly performance trends
Enhance personalized marketing approaches to improve conversion efficiency
